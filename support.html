Common questions

How do I book an artist?

Open the calendar, add a set, pick a date and time, then assign an artist from your venue's lineup and send the request.

How do I get discovered as an artist?

Complete your profile and keep your availability up to date. Managers browsing for talent can find and add you to their lineup.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and choose Delete Account. This anonymizes your data and cancels active bookings. You can also email us to request deletion.

Who can use Nexgig?

Nexgig is for venue managers and performing artists aged 18 and over. Both use the same app.
